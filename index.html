Research Interest:

My interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning. Generally, I am interested in:
  • Efficient inference methods: context compression (RECOMP) and selective attention (RefreshKV).
  • Synthetic data generation: through self-evolving agentic pipeline (SAGE) and privacy-preserving RL (DP-RFT).
  • Evaluation and analysis for realistic tasks involving: long-form outputs, human-model interaction and under-represented user groups.
